Kathmandu Entrance is to prepare students for Bachelor level CMAT entrance Examination in free of cost with best teaching faculties for anyone, anywhere.

Kathmandu Entrance is a learning center that was founded in 2021 A.D. In this platform, we conduct entrance preparation for CMAT and KUUMAT exams. After +2 Level, to read any bachelor faculty in management field as BBA, BIM, BBM, BCA, BTTM and BHM you need to give CMAT or KUUMAT entrance exam to study in Tribhuvan University or Kathmandu University respectively. Through Kathmandu Entrance, we have daily classes in its premise where we teach all the subjects according to CMAT/KUUMAT syllabus. We have separate classes for subjects as English, Logical Reasoning, General Awareness and Math. For the students who are willing to go abroad for their further study.
